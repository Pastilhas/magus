I started my programming journey when I was very young, and yet, already interested in how video games worked. Regularly, I would make small creations using the ROBLOX engine. Although not very successful, I kept this passion, even when I ingressed university.

Having participated in game jams and developed some game prototypes, I was very happy to fulfill my passion. However, when I attempted a job at a gaming company, I realised the professional side of this was not for me and so I decided to further explore other sides of computing.

In the past few years, I have learned a lot: I built websites with varied stacks (PHP, JS, Python); developed multi-purpose ML applications (Python, Java, JS); created games and graphical libraries (Unity, WebGL); worked in low-level environemts and studied drivers and protocols (C/C++, Rust). More importantly, I did not limit my learning to only technical knowledge, I also practised project and enterprise management.

Since 2020, I have been the busiest. I participated in Erasmus+ in Slovakia, having the opportunity to meet a lot of new people from around the world, learning new languages, and developing my independence and personality. In 2021, I started working at BizzTap as a Backend Developer, working in a Node.js+React stack and developing their API and ElasticSearch database.

After that, I joined INEGI, mainly to develop my Master's Thesis in the application of Augmented Reality in Industrial Equipment. I am currently still working in the output product of this study as well as leading the reasearch and developments of the digitalization team.
